TenderBase is an online procurement platform that allows access to TED tender data in OCDS format. Our aim is to support the work of those processing or controlling procurement data in the CEE region.

TenderBase was created within the TransparenCEE Network by K-Monitor and DeepData Ltd, with the contribution of TechSoup and ePanstwo Foundation.
